Surrealist, Conceptual, Multimedia

As an artist, my work explores the surreal, blending dreams and reality in visuals that enchant and disturb. I use abstract forms and diverse media, from digital art to installations, to provoke deep responses and transcend traditional boundaries. Join me in a world where the familiar becomes peculiar and every detail reveals deeper meanings.

Dream Core

Captures surreal, dream-like scenes with ethereal visuals and soft pastels that suggest a mysterious otherworld.

Liminal Spaces

Depict transitional areas that are eerily deserted, such as empty hallways and abandoned buildings, evoking nostalgia and unease.

Backrooms

Features an endless maze of stark, outdated rooms and spaces, creating a sense of isolation and timelessness.

Weird Core

Presents bizarre and uncanny visuals, blending distorted forms and a mix of retro and modern elements that challenge reality.
